VASUKI NFC OWNER / TEAM CONTROL ROOM

Panel URL:
https://vasukinfc.in/admin-panel.html

Required Render environment variable:
ADMIN_PIN=choose-a-private-strong-pin

Who should receive the PIN:
Only the owner and trusted team members.

The panel can:
- Add a product
- Change price and old price
- Change product image URL
- Change description, category, badge and features
- Temporarily hide a product
- Publish or remove a website update

Every active product automatically receives:
- Unique URL: /product/product-slug
- Product structured data for Google
- Canonical product URL
- Sitemap entry
- Collection listing
- Server-validated checkout price

Persistence:
- With MONGODB_URI configured, catalogue and updates persist in MongoDB.
- Without MongoDB, JSON fallback works but Render filesystem changes may reset after a redeploy.

Security:
- Never commit the real ADMIN_PIN into GitHub or share it with customers.
- The panel is noindex and blocked in robots.txt, but the private PIN is still required.
